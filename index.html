About Me

I’m Jasson Christian Flórez, a Business Analyst and Data Scientist with a strong foundation in finance, operations, and analytics. My work focuses on converting complex data into insight-driven strategies that improve profitability, streamline reporting, and drive sustainable business growth.

With hands-on experience across retail, ecommerce, and service industries, I’ve designed performance dashboards, automated reporting systems, and unified financial data environments that bring clarity to leadership decisions. I thrive at the intersection of business intelligence and data engineering — bridging the gap between strategy and execution.

Technically, I’m skilled in SQL, Python, Tableau, Power BI, and Excel, with experience integrating systems such as NetSuite, Oracle, and MySQL. I’ve built forecasting models, e-commerce analytics dashboards, and healthcare outcome trackers — always focused on creating measurable value.

I’m passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful stories that empower better decisions, improve efficiency, and inspire innovation. My goal is simple: to make data tell the truth about performance — clearly, beautifully, and with impact.

Housing Affordability Dashboard

An interactive web application that measures and visualizes housing affordability across regions. The Affordability Score is calculated by comparing median household income to average housing costs, revealing where living is most or least attainable. The dashboard empowers users to explore housing data through dynamic visualizations and real-time comparisons.
